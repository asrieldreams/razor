⚡ Electro Razor – Team Compositions

Aggravate, Overload, and Electro-charged teams that bring out Razor’s storm-wolf potential.

← Back to Electro Guide

Team 1: Aggravate Core

  • Razor
  • Nahida – Fast Dendro application + EM buff
  • Fischl – Off-field Electro + energy battery
  • Flex Slot – Healer / Shielder

Why it works: Consistent Electro-Dendro interaction for Aggravate. Fischl supports uptime and energy, Nahida boosts EM

Disadvantages:

Team 2: Double Electro Hyperbloom

  • Razor
  • Nahida / Collei – Dendro Applicator
  • Xingqiu – Hydro applicator for Bloom cores
  • Kuki Shinobu – Additional Electro for Hyperbloom + healing

Why it works: Razor’s Electro triggers Hyperbloom. Kuki supports healing while Collei and Xingqiu generate bloom cores.

Disadvantages: Inconsistent trigger control > Razor may not always land the Hyperblooms if positioning and rotation aren’t tight.

Team 3: Electro-Charged

  • Razor
  • Xingqiu – Hydro swords for EC
  • Sucrose – Swirl + EM boost + crowd control
  • Barbara – Sustained healing + Hydro aura

Why it works: Razor constantly triggers EC reactions while Sucrose boosts EM and controls groups. Barbara and Xingqiu sustain Hydro uptime.

Disadvantages: EC isn't Razor’s strongest scaling option; lacks DEF shred or shielding.

Team 4: Overload (Pyro DPS)

  • Razor
  • Xiangling – Pyronado + fast Pyro application
  • C6 Bennett – Pyro Infusion + ATK buff + Healer
  • Cheveruse – Electro and Pyro Shred, ATK buff, healing.

Why it works: Overload explosions deal AoE and disrupt enemies. Bennett and Cheveruse provide heavy reaction potential with buffing and healing.

Disadvantages: Overload knockback can scatter enemies, disrupting combos.

Team 5: Overload (Reaction)

  • Razor – Turns into a Pyro-Electro Overload machine
  • C6 Bennett – Pyro infusion + ATK buff + Healer
  • C6 Fischl – Off-field Electro procs for every swing
  • Sucrose or Kazuha – EM boost and grouping

Why it works: Razor’s infused Pyro Normal Attacks from Bennett C6 plus Oz = nonstop Overloads. Sucrose raises EM

Disadvantages: Reaction-based only; no Electro/Physical bonuses. Knockback-heavy and chaotic in tighter fights.

Team 6: Chaos

  • Razor
  • C6 Bennett – Pyro infusion + ATK buff + Healer
  • Xingqiu – Hydro applicator for Bloom cores
  • Nahida – EM boost and Dendro Applicator

Why it works: Entirely EM focused.
