🐺 Overview

Physical Razor thrives on unleashing uninterrupted Normal Attack chains boosted by Superconduct and his wolf-infused Burst. With buffs from Pale Flame and the right setup, he can shred through enemies with relentless efficiency.

  • Build Type: On-field Physical DPS
  • Main Stats: CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG, Physical DMG Bonus, ATK%
  • Core Reaction: Superconduct (Cryo + Electro = Physical RES shred)
  • Best Teams: Razor + Rosaria/Diona + Fischl + Zhongli or Bennett
