📊 Electro Razor – Talent Priority

Which talents to level first for Aggravate-focused Electro DPS builds and why.

← Back to Electro Guide

1️⃣ Elemental Burst: Lightning Fang

  • Priority: ★★★★★ (Top Priority)
  • Why: Razor’s Burst summons the wolf spirit that deals Electro DMG alongside him. This is the core of his reactions potential.
  • Tip: Level this first to maximize Electro application and DPS.

2️⃣ Elemental Skill: Claw and Thunder

  • Priority: ★★★★☆ (High Priority)
  • Why: Generates energy and deals high Electro DMG that can trigger reactions. The hold version also does more damage, deals AoE and refunds energy from stacks.
  • Tip: Level this second to boost your reaction damage and energy generation.

3️⃣ Normal Attack: Steel Fang

  • Priority: ★★☆☆☆ (Low Priority)
  • Why: Still useful during Burst, but Electro Razor relies more on reaction damage than raw Normal Attack scaling.
  • Tip: Level this last unless you’re running a hybrid build with Physical infusion.
