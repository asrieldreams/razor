🌲 Character Overview

Razor is a 4★ Electro Claymore user from Mondstadt, raised by wolves in the forests of Wolvendom. Though human by birth, he considers the wolves his true family—his “Lupical.” Taught swordsmanship by Grand Master Varka and mentored in Electro by Lisa, Razor walks the line between beast and boy, guided by instinct and loyalty.

  • Birthday: September 9
  • Region: Mondstadt
  • Weapon: Claymore
  • Element: Electro
  • Constellation: Lupus Minor

🔮 Passive Talents

1️⃣ Awakening (Ascension 1)

  • Decreases Claw and Thunder’s cooldown by 18%.
  • Using Lightning Fang resets Claw and Thunder’s cooldown.

2️⃣ Hunger (Ascension 4)

  • When Razor’s Energy is below 50%, increases Energy Recharge by 30%.

3️⃣ Wolvensprint (Utility Passive)

  • Decreases sprinting Stamina consumption for your party by 20%.
  • Does not stack with similar effects.

✨ Constellations – Lupus Minor

C1 – Wolf’s Instinct

  • Picking up an Elemental Orb or Particle increases Razor’s DMG by 10% for 8s.
  • Impact: High – consistent damage boost with frequent uptime.

C2 – Suppression

  • Increases CRIT Rate by 10% against enemies with less than 30% HP.
  • Impact: Low – situational and only useful in finishing phases.

C3 – Soul Companion

  • Increases Lightning Fang (Elemental Burst) level by 3 (max 15).
  • Impact: Medium – boosts Electro damage and ATK SPD during Burst.

C4 – Bite

  • Claw and Thunder (Press) reduces enemy DEF by 15% for 7s.
  • Impact: High – synergizes with C1 and boosts team damage.

C5 – Sharpened Claws

  • Increases Claw and Thunder (Elemental Skill) level by 3 (max 15).
  • Impact: Medium – improves Electro damage and energy generation.

C6 – Lupus Fulguris

  • Every 10s, Razor’s next Normal Attack releases lightning for 100% ATK as Electro DMG.
  • When not using Lightning Fang, lightning strikes grant an Electro Sigil.
  • Impact: Low – minor DPS increase; more utility than power spike.
