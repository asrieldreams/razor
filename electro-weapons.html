⚡ Electro Razor – Weapon Analysis

Compare Razor’s best weapons for Electro DPS builds, with pros and cons for each.

← Back to Electro Guide

★★★★★ 5-Star Weapons

Beacon of the Reed Sea

  • Benefit: CRIT Rate + ATK buff after Elemental Skill use.
  • Drawback: Requires HP loss or shield uptime to trigger passive consistently.

Wolf’s Gravestone

  • Benefit: +20% ATK boost, extra +40% when enemies are below 30% HP; teamwide buff.
  • Drawback: No CRIT or EM stats; not tailored for reaction damage.

Skyward Pride

  • Benefit: Energy Recharge + vacuum blade passive; helps maintain Burst uptime.
  • Drawback: No CRIT or EM; passive is underwhelming for reaction builds.

★★★★ 4-Star Weapons

Serpent Spine (Battle Pass)

  • Benefit: CRIT Rate + stacking DMG bonus; Razor’s long field time makes it shine.
  • Drawback: Loses stacks when hit; requires shield support for consistency.

Tidal Shadow

  • Benefit: High base ATK and conditional ATK buff after healing; decent for hybrid builds.
  • Drawback: Requires healing to activate passive; not ideal for self-sufficient Razor.

Ultimate Overlord’s Mega Magic Sword

  • Benefit: Gives Energy Recharge and ATK percent.
  • Drawback: Gives extra ATK based on the number of Melusines you have helped.

★★★ 3-Star Weapons (Early Game)

Skyrider Greatsword

  • Benefit: ATK stacking on hit; usable in early-game Electro builds.
  • Drawback: Low base stats; quickly outclassed by 4★ options.

Debate Club

  • Benefit: Bonus AoE DMG after Elemental Skill; fun for casual play.
  • Drawback: Weak scaling; not viable past early AR levels.

Bloodtainted Greatsword

  • Benefit: Gives EM, Bonus DMG against Electro/Pyro-affected enemies.
  • Drawback: Low Base ATK.
