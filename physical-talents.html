📊 Physical Razor – Talent Priority

Which talents to level first for maximum damage output and why.

← Back to Physical Guide

1️⃣ Normal Attack: Steel Fang

  • Priority: ★★★★★ (Top Priority)
  • Why: Razor’s primary source of damage. His Normal Attacks scale decently well and are further enhanced by his Elemental Burst.
  • Tip: Level this first and as high as possible > it’s the backbone of his Physical DPS playstyle.

2️⃣ Elemental Burst: Lightning Fang

  • Priority: ★★★★☆ (High Priority)
  • Why: Grants increased ATK SPD, and a companion wolf that deals Electro DMG alongside Razor’s attacks.
  • Tip: Level this second > it boosts Razor’s DPS and adds Electro to his Physical kit.

3️⃣ Elemental Skill: Claw and Thunder

  • Priority: ★★★☆☆ (Optional Priority)
  • Why: Generates energy and triggers Pale Flame stacks. Tap for quick energy; hold for AoE and more energy particles.
  • Tip: Level this last unless you’re struggling with energy generation or using Razor in a hybrid build.
