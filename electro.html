🌩️ Overview

Electro Razor trades brute force for lightning-fast agility and reaction damage. By focusing on Aggravate with Dendro teams or Overload with C6 Bennett, Razor becomes a highly active, burst-driven DPS with shocking style.

  • Build Type: On-field Electro DPS (Aggravate / Overload)
  • Main Stats: EM, Electro DMG, CRIT
  • Core Reaction: Aggravate or Overload (optional EC / Hyperbloom tech)
  • Best Teams: Razor + Nahida + Fischl + Bennett (Aggravate Core)
